Our Mission

  • To provide quality Catholic education anchored on Augustinian spirituality
  • To promote academic excellence and critical thinking
  • To foster responsible citizenship and environmental stewardship
  • To build a strong, value-driven community that embraces both tradition and innovation

Why Choose Us?

We offer a faith-based formation grounded in the teachings of the Catholic Church, ensuring academic excellence across all levels. Our innovative learning approach integrates technology and flexible education, while our student-centered programs nurture leadership, creativity, and core values. We are also deeply committed to civic engagement through meaningful community outreach and social responsibility.

Colegio San Agustin provides a complete educational journey, offering programs from Preschool, Grade School, Junior High School, and Senior High School—featuring both Academic and Technical-Vocational Tracks—to a variety of College Courses, which may vary depending on the campus location.
