Welcome to Colegio San Agustin

"Caritas. Sapientia. Scientia." — Charity. Wisdom. Knowledge.

Colegio San Agustin is a private Catholic educational institution committed to the holistic development of students through faith-based and academically excellent education. Rooted in the Augustinian values of love, truth, and community, our school fosters an environment where every learner is empowered to become a servant-leader for society. .

Founded with the mission to nurture Christian values and academic excellence, Colegio San Agustin stands as a beacon of quality education in the region. We offer a complete learning journey—from Preschool, Elementary, Junior High, Senior High, to College—guided by the principles of St. Augustine, our patron saint. Our campus is a safe, inclusive, and learner-centered environment that supports both academic achievement and moral formation. With competent and compassionate faculty, modern facilities, and a dynamic curriculum, we ensure that our students are well-equipped for success in both life and vocation.
